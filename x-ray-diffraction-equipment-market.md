<p><p>X-ray diffraction (XRD) equipment is primarily categorized into two market types: powder XRD and single-crystal XRD. Powder XRD is used for analyzing crystalline materials in bulk form, providing average structural information and phase identification. It is widely employed in materials science and chemistry. In contrast, single-crystal XRD focuses on determining the precise arrangement of atoms in a single crystal, yielding detailed structural insights essential for understanding molecular interactions and properties in various applications, including pharmaceuticals and materials research.</p></p>
