<p><ul><li>Aluminum Auto Radiator</li><li>Copper Auto Radiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto radiator market comprises primarily two types: aluminum and copper radiators. Aluminum radiators are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and offer efficient heat dissipation, making them popular in modern vehicles. They are often preferred for their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. In contrast, copper radiators are known for their superior thermal conductivity and durability, providing enhanced performance in high-demand conditions. While copper is heavier and generally more expensive, it remains a choice for some performance applications and classic vehicles.</p></p>
