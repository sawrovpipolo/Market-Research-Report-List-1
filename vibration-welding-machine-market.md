This process generates frictional heat at the joint interface, allowing the materials to melt and fuse together. <p><strong>The Vibration Welding Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear</li><li>Orbital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vibration welding machine market can be categorized into two main types: linear and orbital. Linear vibration welding involves oscillating the parts in a straight line, creating frictional heat at the interface for strong, durable bonds. In contrast, orbital vibration welding uses a circular motion, which allows for more complex geometries and ensures even distribution of heat across the weld area. Both types are widely used in manufacturing for joining thermoplastic materials, enhancing production efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
