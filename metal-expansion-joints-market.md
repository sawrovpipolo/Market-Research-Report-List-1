<p><strong>The Metal Expansion Joints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Expansion Joints</li><li>Angular Expansion Joints</li><li>Lateral Expansion Joints</li><li>Universal Expansion Joints</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal expansion joints market comprises various types designed to accommodate thermal expansion and movement in piping systems. Axial expansion joints allow movement along their length, suitable for absorbing straight-line thermal expansion. Angular expansion joints manage angular misalignments and absorb rotational movements. Lateral expansion joints accommodate sideways movement, ideal for offset piping systems. Universal expansion joints combine the features of axial and lateral joints, allowing for multidirectional movement, making them versatile for complex installations. Together, these joints enhance system flexibility and longevity.</p></p>
