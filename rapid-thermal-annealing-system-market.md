<p><strong>The Rapid Thermal Annealing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lamp-based</li><li>Laser-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rapid Thermal Annealing (RTA) System market is primarily segmented into lamp-based and laser-based types. Lamp-based RTA utilizes high-intensity lamps to quickly heat semiconductor wafers, promoting rapid thermal processing and improving material properties. This method is known for its uniform heating and high throughput. In contrast, laser-based RTA employs focused laser beams to achieve precise, localized heating on the substrate, allowing for excellent control over temperature profiles. This technique is often used for specialized applications requiring fine-tuning of material characteristics.</p></p>
