<p><strong>The High Temperature Filter Media Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PPS</li><li>P84</li><li>PTFE</li><li>Nomex</li><li>PSA</li><li>Fiber Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high temperature filter media market includes various types tailored for extreme thermal conditions. PPS (Polyphenylene Sulfide) offers chemical resistance and durability, while P84 (polyimide) excels in high thermal stability. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is known for its exceptional non-stick properties and chemical resistance. Nomex, a meta-aramid fiber, is commonly used for its heat resistance. PSA (Polymer Styrene Adhesive) provides adhesion properties in certain applications. Fiberglass filters are recognized for their strength and thermal performance, making them suitable for industrial and environmental applications.</p></p>
