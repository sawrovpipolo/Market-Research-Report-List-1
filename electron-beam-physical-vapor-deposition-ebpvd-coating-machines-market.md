Single electron gun systems utilize a single source for vapor deposition, ideal for smaller-scale applications or specific coatings. In contrast, multiple electron gun systems enable simultaneous use of various electron beams, allowing for more complex coatings and higher throughput, making them suitable for large-scale production or diverse material requirements.</p></p>
