<p><p>The DMLS (Direct Metal Laser Sintering) 3D printers market is segmented into various types, with coaxial powder feeding and roller powder feeding being prominent. Coaxial powder feeding involves delivering metal powder directly alongside the laser beam, ensuring uniform melting and efficient material usage. In contrast, roller powder feeding utilizes a roller system to spread a consistent layer of powder across the build platform, enabling rapid layer formation. Both methods improve build efficiency and part quality in metal additive manufacturing.</p></p>
