<p><strong>The Ternary Precursor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NCM Type</li><li>NCA Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ternary precursor markets encompass two primary types: NCM (Nickel Cobalt Manganese) and NCA (Nickel Cobalt Aluminum). The NCM type utilizes a combination of nickel, cobalt, and manganese to enhance battery efficiency and longevity, making it popular in electric vehicles. In contrast, the NCA type relies on nickel, cobalt, and aluminum, offering higher energy density and improved performance, particularly for high-demand applications. Both types are crucial in advancing lithium-ion battery technology and catering to the growing energy storage market.</p></p>
