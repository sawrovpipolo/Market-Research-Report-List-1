<p><p>Superabrasive disc tools are categorized primarily into two types based on their abrasive materials: diamond and cubic boron nitride (CBN). Diamond materials are ideal for hard materials like ceramics and composites, offering superior cutting efficiency and durability. In contrast, CBN materials excel in grinding ferrous metals, providing exceptional wear resistance and thermal stability. Both types are essential in precision machining, with applications ranging from automotive to aerospace, enhancing productivity and surface finish quality in various manufacturing processes.</p></p>
