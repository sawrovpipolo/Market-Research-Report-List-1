<p><strong>The RFID Handheld Terminal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LF RFID System</li><li>HF RFID System</li><li>UHF RFID System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID handheld terminal market is segmented into three primary types based on frequency: LF (Low Frequency) RFID systems, HF (High Frequency) RFID systems, and UHF (Ultra High Frequency) RFID systems. LF RFID operates at 125-134 kHz, ideal for access control and animal tracking. HF RFID, operating at 13.56 MHz, is commonly used for smart cards and library systems. UHF RFID, spanning 900 MHz, offers longer read ranges, making it suitable for supply chain and inventory management. Additionally, there are other specialized RFID systems catering to unique applications.</p></p>
