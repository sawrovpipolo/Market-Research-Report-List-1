<p><p>The Enhanced Fire Protection Systems market encompasses two primary types: active and passive systems. Active systems involve mechanisms that detect and mitigate fires, such as sprinklers, alarms, and fire extinguishing systems, which are triggered by fire conditions. In contrast, passive systems focus on preventing fire spread through design features, including fire-resistant materials, compartmentalization, and smoke barriers. Together, these systems enhance safety, compliance, and risk management in various environments, ensuring comprehensive fire protection strategies.</p></p>
