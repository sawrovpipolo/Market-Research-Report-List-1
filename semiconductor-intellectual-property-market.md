<p><ul><li>Hard IP Cores</li><li>Soft IP Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Semiconductor Intellectual Property (IP) market consists mainly of two types: Hard IP Cores and Soft IP Cores. Hard IP Cores are pre-designed, ready-to-manufacture components, typically in a fixed format, offering faster integration but less flexibility. They often include complex functions, such as processors or interfaces, optimized for power and performance. Soft IP Cores, conversely, are synthesizable designs that can be customized for specific applications, providing greater flexibility but requiring more design effort and time for implementation.</p></p>
