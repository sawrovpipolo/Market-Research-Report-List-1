DCC focuses on long-term contracts for exclusive transportation services tailored to a client's needs. DTM provides dedicated fleets for specific clients, emphasizing efficiency and reliability. ITM integrates logistics processes with advanced technology solutions, enhancing visibility and decision-making. Additionally, the logistics software market offers tools and platforms that optimize logistics operations, improving supply chain efficiency and reducing costs.</p></p>
