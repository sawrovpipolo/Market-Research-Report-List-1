<p><ul><li>High-acuity Monitors</li><li>Mid-acuity Monitors</li><li>Low-acuity Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-parameter patient monitoring equipment market is categorized into high-acuity, mid-acuity, and low-acuity monitors. High-acuity monitors are designed for critically ill patients, offering advanced features like continuous ECG monitoring, invasive blood pressure, and multi-channel capabilities. Mid-acuity monitors serve patients with moderate health issues, typically used in general wards, providing essential vital sign tracking. Low-acuity monitors are suitable for stable patients in outpatient settings, focusing on basic vital signs with minimal functionality, ensuring widespread accessibility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
