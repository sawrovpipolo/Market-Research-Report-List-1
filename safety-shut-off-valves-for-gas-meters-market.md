<p><p>Safety shut-off valves for gas meters are critical components that ensure the safe operation of gas distribution systems. The market primarily includes motorised valves and solenoid valves. Motorised valves are electrically actuated, allowing for remote control and automation, making them suitable for larger systems. In contrast, solenoid valves operate via electromagnetic coils, providing quick and reliable shut-off in response to safety alarms. Both types enhance safety by preventing gas leaks and ensuring efficient gas flow management in residential and industrial settings.</p></p>
