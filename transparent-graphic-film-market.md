<p><p>The transparent graphic film market is categorized mainly into four types: PVC (polyvinyl chloride), PP (polypropylene), PE (polyethylene), and others. PVC is widely used for its durability and print quality, making it ideal for signage and displays. PP offers a lightweight alternative with good chemical resistance, suitable for packaging and labels. PE is valued for its flexibility and moisture resistance, often used in outdoor applications. The "Others" category includes various specialized films catering to niche markets and applications.</p></p>
