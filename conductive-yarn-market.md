<p><p>The Conductive Yarn Market includes three primary types: Metallic, Carbon-Based, and Metal Compound. Metallic yarns use metals like silver or copper for high conductivity, ideal for smart textiles and wearables. Carbon-Based yarns incorporate materials like graphene or carbon nanotubes, offering lightweight and flexible solutions for sensors and EMI shielding. Metal Compound yarns blend metals with polymers or coatings, balancing conductivity and durability for applications in medical devices and industrial textiles. Each type caters to specific performance and application needs.</p></p>
