<p><strong>The GNSS Signal Simulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel Simulator</li><li>Multi Channel Simulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GNSS signal simulator market comprises two main types: single-channel simulators and multi-channel simulators. Single-channel simulators are designed to generate signals for one satellite, primarily used for testing individual receiver functions and software. In contrast, multi-channel simulators can emulate multiple satellite signals simultaneously, allowing for comprehensive testing of GNSS receivers in complex scenarios. This capability is essential for assessing performance under various conditions, making multi-channel simulators more versatile and beneficial for advanced research and development in GNSS technology.</p></p>
