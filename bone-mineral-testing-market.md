<p><strong>The Bone & Mineral Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central DXA</li><li>Peripheral DXA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bone & Mineral Testing market includes Central DXA and Peripheral DXA as key types of testing methods. Central DXA (Dual-energy X-ray Absorptiometry) is primarily used in clinical settings to assess bone density in major skeletal sites, providing comprehensive data for osteoporosis diagnosis. Peripheral DXA, on the other hand, measures bone density in peripheral sites like the wrist or heel, often serving as a quick screening tool. Both methods are essential for managing bone health and preventing fractures.</p></p>
