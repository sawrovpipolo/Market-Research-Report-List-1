<p><strong>The Snow Melting Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HydronicSnowmeltSystems</li><li>ElectricSnowmeltSystems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The snow melting controllers market is primarily divided into two types: hydronic snow melting systems and electric snow melting systems. Hydronic systems use heated water circulated through pipes beneath the surface, effectively melting snow and ice. These systems are energy-efficient and ideal for large areas. Electric snow melting systems, on the other hand, employ electric heating cables or mats installed under the pavement to generate heat. They provide quick and efficient snow removal but can be more costly to operate. Both systems enhance safety and convenience in winter conditions.</p></p>
