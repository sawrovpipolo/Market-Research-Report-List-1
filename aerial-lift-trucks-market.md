<p><p>Aerial lift trucks encompass various types designed for elevated work access. Scissor lifts feature a crisscrossing support structure, providing stable vertical lifts suitable for indoor tasks. Boom lifts, featuring extendable arms, offer both vertical and horizontal reach, ideal for construction and maintenance projects. Telehandlers, a hybrid of a forklift and boom lift, can lift heavy loads to significant heights and maneuver materials on rough terrain, making them versatile for agricultural and industrial settings. Each type serves distinct operational needs and environments.</p></p>
