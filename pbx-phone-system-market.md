<p><ul><li>Traditional PBX/Analogue PBX</li><li>Hosted PBX</li><li>VoIP/IP PBX</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PBX phone system market includes three main types: Traditional PBX/Analogue PBX, Hosted PBX, and VoIP/IP PBX. Traditional PBX relies on physical hardware and standard telephone lines for communication, typically suited for larger enterprises. Hosted PBX, also known as cloud PBX, is managed off-site by a service provider, offering cost-effectiveness and scalability for businesses of all sizes. VoIP/IP PBX leverages internet protocols for voice communication, enabling advanced features and flexibility, making it popular among modern organizations.</p></p>
