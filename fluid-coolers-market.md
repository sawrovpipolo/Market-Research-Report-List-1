<p><ul><li>Evaporative Coolers</li><li>Dry Air Cooler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluid coolers market comprises two main types: evaporative coolers and dry air coolers. Evaporative coolers utilize the cooling effect of water evaporation to lower air temperature, making them energy-efficient and environmentally friendly. They are ideal for hot, dry climates. In contrast, dry air coolers use air-cooled heat exchangers to dissipate heat without water, suitable for scenarios where water availability is limited. Both types cater to industrial and commercial applications, enhancing system efficiency and thermal management.</p></p>
