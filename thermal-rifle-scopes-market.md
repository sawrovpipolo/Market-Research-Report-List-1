<p><p>The thermal rifle scopes market is primarily divided into two types: un-cooled thermal imaging and cryogenically cooled thermal imaging. Un-cooled thermal imaging scopes utilize sensors that operate at room temperature, making them more compact, lightweight, and cost-effective. In contrast, cryogenically cooled thermal imaging scopes use advanced cooling technologies to enhance sensitivity and resolution, delivering superior image quality in low-light conditions. These scopes are typically used in professional and military applications where precision and clarity are critical.</p></p>
