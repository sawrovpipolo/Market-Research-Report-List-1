<p><p>The hydraulic shock damper market is primarily categorized into two types: adjustable and non-adjustable shock absorbers. Adjustable shock absorbers allow users to modify the damping characteristics, providing customizable performance for various driving conditions and preferences. Non-adjustable shock absorbers, on the other hand, come pre-set and offer a fixed level of damping, delivering consistent performance without the need for user intervention. Both types serve to enhance vehicle stability and comfort by minimizing the impact of road irregularities.</p></p>
