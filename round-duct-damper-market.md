<p><ul><li>Single Blade</li><li>Multi-blade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The round duct damper market is primarily categorized into two types: single blade and multi-blade dampers. Single blade dampers consist of a single, pivoting blade that efficiently regulates airflow in circular ducts, offering a compact and cost-effective solution. In contrast, multi-blade dampers feature multiple blades that allow for enhanced airflow control and distribution, making them suitable for larger systems requiring greater precision. Both types serve essential roles in HVAC systems, improving efficiency and comfort in various applications.</p></p>
