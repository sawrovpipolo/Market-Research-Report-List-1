<p><ul><li>Torque Only Sensor(TOS)</li><li>Torque Angle Sensor(TAS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) Sensor market comprises two main types: Torque Only Sensor (TOS) and Torque Angle Sensor (TAS). TOS measures the steering torque applied by the driver, providing direct feedback to help adjust the level of assistance offered by the EPS system. In contrast, TAS measures both the steering torque and angle, allowing for enhanced precision in vehicle response and stability. Both sensor types contribute to improving driving comfort and safety by optimizing steering control.</p></p>
