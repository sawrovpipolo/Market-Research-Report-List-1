<p><ul><li>Die Casting</li><li>Permanent Mold Casting</li><li>Sand Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum castings market comprises several key types: Die Casting, Permanent Mold Casting, and Sand Casting. Die Casting involves forcing molten aluminum into a mold at high pressure, producing intricate shapes with excellent surface finishes. Permanent Mold Casting uses reusable molds, allowing for better dimensional accuracy and surface quality, often used for medium-volume production. Sand Casting, the most versatile method, involves creating molds from sand, suitable for large and complex parts but with lower precision and surface finish compared to the other methods.</p></p>
