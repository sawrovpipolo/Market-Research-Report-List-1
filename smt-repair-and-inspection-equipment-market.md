<p><p>The SMT Repair and Inspection Equipment market consists of three primary types: Automated Optical Inspection (AOI), Solder Paste Inspection (SPI), and Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI). AOI utilizes high-resolution cameras to detect defects in surface-mounted components on PCBs. SPI measures solder paste deposition to ensure proper application before component placement. AXI employs X-ray technology to inspect internal features of assembled PCBs, identifying hidden defects. Together, these technologies enhance quality control, yield, and reliability in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
