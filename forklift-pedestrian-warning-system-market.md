<p><p>The Forklift Pedestrian Warning System market comprises primarily two technology types: RFID-based and UWB-based systems. RFID-based systems utilize radio-frequency identification tags to detect the presence of pedestrians near forklifts, providing alerts to mitigate collisions. In contrast, UWB-based systems employ ultra-wideband technology for precise localization and tracking, enabling real-time distance measurement between forklifts and pedestrians. Both systems enhance workplace safety by reducing accident risks, optimizing operational efficiency, and fostering a safer environment in industrial settings.</p></p>
