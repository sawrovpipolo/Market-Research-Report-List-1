<p><strong>The Food Pesticide Residue Testing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GC, GCMS, GCMSMS</li><li>LC, LCMS, LCMSMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food pesticide residue testing equipment market includes several analytical techniques to detect pesticide residues in food products. GC (Gas Chromatography) is used for volatile compounds, while GC-MS (Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry) combines separation and identification, enhancing sensitivity and specificity. GC-MS-MS (tandem mass spectrometry) offers even greater sensitivity for complex samples. LC (Liquid Chromatography) is suited for non-volatile substances, with LC-MS and LC-MS-MS providing robust methods for quantitative and qualitative analysis, improving detection limits and accuracy in food safety testing.</p></p>
