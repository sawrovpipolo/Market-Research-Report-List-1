<p><p>The medical grade surge protector market is categorized into three types: Voltage Switch Type, which activates to disconnect electrical loads during voltage surges; Pressure Limiting Type, which mitigates surge impacts by using pressure-responsive components; and Combination Type, which integrates both voltage switching and pressure limiting features for enhanced protection. These surge protectors ensure the safety and reliability of medical devices by safeguarding them against electrical anomalies, thereby maintaining operational integrity in critical healthcare environments.</p></p>
