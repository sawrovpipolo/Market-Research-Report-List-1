Rigid foam is characterized by its high density and structural integrity, making it ideal for applications like insulation in construction, refrigeration, and packaging. In contrast, flexible foam is softer and more adaptable, commonly used in furniture, bedding, automotive interiors, and cushioning materials. Both types offer unique properties catering to various industries, driving innovation and demand in the PU foam market.</p></p>
