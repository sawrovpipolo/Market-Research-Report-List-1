<p><p>The polyester composite geogrid market consists of two main types: biaxial tension and uniaxial tension geogrids. Biaxial tension geogrids are designed to provide stability and reinforcement in multiple directions, making them suitable for applications like base stabilization and slope support. In contrast, uniaxial tension geogrids are engineered for high tensile strength in one direction, typically used in retaining walls and soil reinforcement. Both types offer enhanced performance in civil engineering and construction projects, contributing to improved durability and load distribution.</p></p>
