<p><p>The dental X-ray machines market is segmented into ordinary X-rays and panoramic X-rays. Ordinary X-rays provide detailed images of individual teeth and surrounding structures, assisting in diagnosing cavities, infections, and bone loss. In contrast, panoramic X-rays capture a comprehensive view of the entire mouth in a single image, including the jaw, teeth, and sinuses, aiding in treatment planning and orthodontics. Both types are essential for effective dental care, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
