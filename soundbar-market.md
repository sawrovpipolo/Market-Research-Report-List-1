The 2.1 channel adds a subwoofer, enhancing bass responses and providing a richer audio experience for movies and music. The 5.1 channel includes five speakers and a subwoofer, creating surround sound that immerses listeners in a multi-dimensional audio environment, making it suitable for home theater enthusiasts. Each type caters to varying needs and preferences.</p></p>
