<p><p>The HiToxic Gas Detector market is primarily segmented into fixed and portable gas detectors. Fixed gas detectors are installed in specific locations, providing continuous surveillance and immediate alerts in industrial or commercial settings. They are designed for permanent monitoring of potential gas leaks and are typically connected to a central alarm system. Portable gas detectors, on the other hand, are handheld devices used for spot-checking or in confined spaces.
