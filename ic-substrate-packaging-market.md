<p><p>IC substrate packaging encompasses various materials, including metal, ceramics, and glass, each serving specific applications. Metal substrates offer excellent thermal conductivity and are often used in power electronics. Ceramic substrates provide high thermal stability and electrical insulation, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. Glass substrates are known for their superior dielectric properties and mechanical strength, making them ideal for advanced packaging solutions. Each material type contributes uniquely to the performance, reliability, and efficiency of integrated circuits in electronic devices.</p></p>
