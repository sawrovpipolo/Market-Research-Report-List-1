<p><p>UV absorbers are chemical compounds used to protect materials from the harmful effects of ultraviolet (UV) radiation. They are widely utilized in industries such as plastics, coatings, adhesives, personal care, and textiles to prevent degradation, discoloration, and loss of mechanical properties caused by UV exposure. Common types of UV absorbers include benzophenones, benzotriazoles, and triazines, each offering specific benefits depending on the application.</p><p>The UV absorbers market is experiencing steady growth, driven by increasing demand for durable and UV-resistant materials across various industries. Overall, the UV absorbers market is poised for robust growth, supported by technological advancements and expanding applications.</p></p>
