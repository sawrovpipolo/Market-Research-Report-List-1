<p><p>The Low Pressure Reverse Osmosis Pumps market comprises several types, including centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize rotational energy to move water, offering high flow rates and efficiency, making them ideal for large-scale applications. In contrast, positive displacement pumps function by trapping a fixed volume of water and forcing it through the system, ensuring consistent pressure and flow rates. Other market types may include specialized or hybrid designs that cater to specific operational needs within reverse osmosis systems.</p></p>
