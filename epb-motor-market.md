<p><p>The EPB motor market is primarily divided into two types: DC brushed motors and DC brushless motors. DC brushed motors, characterized by their simple design and cost-effectiveness, utilize brushes for commutation, making them easy to control but subject to wear over time. In contrast, DC brushless motors employ electronic commutation, offering higher efficiency, longer lifespan, and lower maintenance. They are increasingly favored in applications requiring precision and reliability, driving growth in sectors like automotive and consumer electronics.</p></p>
