<p><strong>The Radiant Electric Floor Heating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Hydronic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiant electric floor heating market consists of two main types: electric and hydronic systems. Electric systems utilize heating cables or mats installed beneath the flooring, efficiently warming the space through radiant heat. They are often easier to install and ideal for smaller areas. In contrast, hydronic systems circulate warm water through pipes beneath the floor, providing consistent and even heating throughout larger spaces. Both systems enhance comfort and energy efficiency, making them popular choices for modern building designs.</p></p>
