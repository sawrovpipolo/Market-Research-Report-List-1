<p><p>Adhesive tape markets are categorized based on backing materials. **PP Backed** tapes use polypropylene, offering durability, moisture resistance, and flexibility, ideal for packaging and sealing. **Paper Backed** tapes, made from paper, are eco-friendly, easy to tear, and printable, commonly used in lightweight packaging and masking. **PVC Backed** tapes, made from polyvinyl chloride, provide excellent insulation, weather resistance, and strength, making them suitable for electrical and industrial applications. Each type caters to specific needs based on material properties and application requirements.</p></p>
