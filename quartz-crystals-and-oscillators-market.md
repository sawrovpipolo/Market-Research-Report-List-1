The market includes several types: </p><p>- SPXO (Standard Parallel Crystal Oscillator) provides basic timing functions with moderate stability. </p><p>- TCXO (Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillator) offers improved stability across temperature variations. </p><p>- VCXO (Voltage Controlled Crystal Oscillator) allows frequency adjustment based on voltage changes. </p><p>- OCXO (Oven Controlled Crystal Oscillator) maintains high precision through temperature regulation. </p><p>Overall, these technologies cater to diverse applications in telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive systems.</p></p>
