<p><p>The disposable surgical drapes market is primarily categorized into nonwoven and woven surgical drapes. Nonwoven surgical drapes are made from synthetic fibers, providing high barrier protection against fluid and microorganisms, while being lightweight and cost-effective. They are typically used for single procedures. In contrast, woven surgical drapes are crafted from traditional textiles, offering durability and reusability, but may not always provide the same level of sterility and fluid resistance. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring a sterile surgical environment.</p></p>
