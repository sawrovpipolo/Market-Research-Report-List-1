<p><p>The synthetic enamel market comprises various types based on the resin used. **Alkyd Resin-Based** enamels are durable, glossy, and ideal for metal and wood surfaces. **Water-Based** enamels are eco-friendly, low-odor, and quick-drying, suitable for indoor applications. **Oil-Based** enamels offer superior adhesion and durability, often used for high-traffic areas. **Nitro-Based** enamels dry quickly and provide a smooth finish, commonly used in automotive and industrial coatings. **Others** include specialized formulations like epoxy or polyurethane-based enamels, catering to unique performance or environmental requirements.</p></p>
