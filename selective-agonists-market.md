<p><p>The selective agonists market comprises several key types based on adrenergic receptor specificity. α1 selective agonists primarily target vascular smooth muscle, promoting vasoconstriction. α2 selective agonists act on the central nervous system, reducing sympathetic nervous activity and modulating neurotransmitter release. β1 selective agonists focus on cardiac tissues, enhancing heart rate and contractility. In contrast, β2 selective agonists are primarily involved in bronchodilation and smooth muscle relaxation, making them vital in respiratory therapies. Each type serves distinct therapeutic needs.</p></p>
