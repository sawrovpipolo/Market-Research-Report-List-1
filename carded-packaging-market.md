<p><strong>The Carded Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoforming</li><li>Cold forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carded packaging market is primarily divided into two types: thermoforming and cold forming. Thermoforming involves heating plastic sheets until pliable, then molding them into desired shapes, commonly used for clamshells and trays that showcase products. Cold forming, on the other hand, uses high pressure to shape materials at room temperature, typically producing blisters and packages with robust barriers, ideal for pharmaceuticals and sensitive products. Both methods enhance product visibility, protection, and shelf-life in a competitive market.</p></p>
