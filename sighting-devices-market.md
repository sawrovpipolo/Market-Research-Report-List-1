<p><p>The sighting devices market includes several types designed for improving aiming accuracy. Telescopic sights use magnification to enhance distant target visibility, making them ideal for hunting and long-range shooting. Collimating optical sights combine a lens and reticle, allowing users to aim quickly while keeping both eyes open, suitable for both firearms and archery. Reflex sights use a parallax-free design with illuminated reticles, enabling rapid target acquisition in dynamic scenarios. Each type caters to different shooting needs and preferences in various environments.</p></p>
