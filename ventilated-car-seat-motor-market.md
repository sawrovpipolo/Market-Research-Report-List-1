<p><strong>The Ventilated Car Seat Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Fan Motor"</li><li>"Blower Motor"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ventilated car seat motor market primarily includes two types: fan motors and blower motors. Fan motors utilize blades to circulate air, providing a gentle airflow that enhances comfort by reducing heat buildup in the seats. In contrast, blower motors employ a more powerful mechanism to push air through vents, offering a stronger airflow that effectively cools the seat surface. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing passenger comfort by improving air circulation and managing seat temperatures during travel.</p></p>
