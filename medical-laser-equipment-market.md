<p><p>The medical laser equipment market encompasses various types of laser systems, each serving distinct applications. Solid state lasers are known for their robustness and versatility, often used in surgical procedures. Gas lasers, which include CO2 lasers, offer precision in soft tissue surgeries. Dye lasers are characterized by their tunability and are frequently employed in dermatology. Semiconductor lasers, also known as diode lasers, provide compact and efficient solutions, ideal for applications like laser hair removal and phototherapy. Together, these technologies enhance medical treatments.</p></p>
