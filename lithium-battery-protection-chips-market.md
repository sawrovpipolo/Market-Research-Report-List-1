<p><p>Lithium battery protection chips are essential for ensuring the safety and longevity of lithium-ion batteries by preventing overvoltage (OV), overtemperature (OT), and overcurrent (OCD) conditions. OV protection chips prevent voltage levels from exceeding safe limits, safeguarding the battery during charging. OT protection chips monitor temperature to avoid overheating, which can lead to thermal runaway. OCD protection chips safeguard against excessive current draw, preventing damage or hazards. Together, these protection mechanisms enhance the reliability and safety of lithium battery operations.</p></p>
