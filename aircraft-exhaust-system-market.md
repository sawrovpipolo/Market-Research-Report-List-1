<p><ul><li>Short Stack System</li><li>Collector System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft exhaust system market features two primary types: Short Stack Systems and Collector Systems. Short Stack Systems are designed for lighter aircraft and provide efficient exhaust expulsion with minimal weight and drag, enhancing performance. In contrast, Collector Systems are typically used in larger and more complex aircraft, where they gather exhaust from multiple cylinders and direct it away efficiently, reducing noise and improving overall engine performance. Both systems play crucial roles in enhancing aircraft efficiency and safety.</p></p>
