<p><p>Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI) is a non-destructive testing method used primarily in electronics manufacturing to inspect solder joints, connections, and components. The market is segmented into 2D AXI and 3D AXI systems. 2D AXI provides flat, two-dimensional images, allowing for quick inspections of anomalies. In contrast, 3D AXI generates volumetric images, offering a more comprehensive analysis of complex assemblies by providing depth information, enhancing defect detection capabilities, and ensuring high quality in production processes.</p></p>
