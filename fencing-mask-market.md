<p><p>The fencing mask market includes three primary types: foil, epee, and sabre. Foil masks are designed for safety during foil fencing, featuring a lightweight construction and a fine mesh for visibility. Epee masks provide enhanced protection for the heavier epee blade, often including a thicker bib. Sabre masks focus on securing the head and neck area, accommodating the quicker, more aggressive nature of sabre fencing. Each mask type is tailored to the unique demands of the respective fencing discipline.</p></p>
