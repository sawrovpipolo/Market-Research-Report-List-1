<p><p>The Repair Tire Sealant market primarily consists of two types: gel and aerosol. Gel sealants are thick, viscous substances that fill punctures and can often provide a long-lasting solution, as they remain in the tire longer. Aerosol sealants, on the other hand, are convenient spray products that quickly seal leaks but may have a shorter lifespan. Both types are designed to offer quick fixes for flat tires, catering to different consumer preferences for ease of use and effectiveness.</p></p>
